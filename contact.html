Frequently Asked Questions

Do you charge any upfront consultancy fees?

No. Angel Credit Services operates with complete transparency. We earn our facilitation fees directly from our partner banks upon successful disbursal. There are zero hidden or upfront fees charged to our clients.

How quickly can I get a loan approved?

Approval times vary based on the loan type and the applicant's profile. We work efficiently to process all applications and aim for the fastest possible turnaround with our partner banks.

Can you help if I have a low CIBIL score?

We partner with a wide spectrum of lending institutions, including NBFCs that are flexible with credit scores. We can guide you on the best options available based on your specific report.
